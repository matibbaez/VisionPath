At **VisionPath**, your data privacy is fundamental. This policy details how we handle your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
We only collect data strictly necessary to operate:
* **Contact Data:** Name, email, and phone number, provided voluntarily when contacting us.
* **Navigation Data:** IP address, device type, and anonymous site usage metrics.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
* Respond to inquiries and send commercial proposals.
* Execute the contracted services.
* Optimize the technical performance of our site.
* **We never sell or share your data for third-party advertising purposes.**

## 3. Cookies
We use essential technical cookies for site operation and analytical cookies to understand web traffic. You can configure your browser to block them, though some functions may be affected.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Our site may integrate third-party tools (such as WhatsApp or Vercel Analytics). We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy policies of such platforms.

## 5. Security
We apply industry security standards (HTTPS, encryption in transit) to protect your information against unauthorized access.

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data from our records. To do so, please contact us at **hola@visionpath.com**.
